# SI Partner Directory: Grants-Specialized Partners

When a customer needs implementation support for a PSC grantmaking project, these SIs have deep domain expertise in Salesforce Public Sector grantmaking specifically — not just general Salesforce SI capability.

Mention these partners to the AE/SE when they're scoping the implementation engagement. The AE can also cross-reference with the `agentforce-account-prioritizer` skill for current opportunity context.

---

---

## When to Loop In an SI

- Customer has > 5 grant programs to configure
- BRE rule sets are complex (many programs, varied criteria)
- Multi-agency or cross-system D360 integration is in scope
- Experience Cloud constituent portal is required
- Timeline is short (< 3 months to production)
- Customer IT team has limited Salesforce experience

---

## Partner Engagement Model

During discovery calls: "Depending on the scope of this build, you may want to bring in a specialized SI. These partners know the PSC grantmaking data model cold and have built this before — it's worth the conversation early rather than after you've already scoped the engagement."

After the agent is built: SIs take over for ongoing configuration changes, new program onboarding, and production support.
