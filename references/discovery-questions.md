# Discovery Question Bank

Extended qualification questions organized by grant lifecycle stage. Use these during Phase 1 discovery and for deeper customer qualification conversations.

---

## Phase 0 / Org Readiness Questions
1. Is PSC Grantmaking installed and licensed in this sandbox?
2. Do you have Agentforce for Public Sector licensed?
3. Is Business Rules Engine (BRE) configured for eligibility checking?
4. Do you have OmniStudio licensed?
5. Is Data 360 for Public Sector licensed in this org?
6. Is Einstein Generative AI enabled (required for Prompt Template actions)?
7. Is Experience Cloud set up (required for public-facing agents)?

---

## Eligibility & Intake Stage
1. How many distinct grant programs do you manage? (Affects BRE rule set count)
2. Are eligibility criteria the same across all programs, or does each program have unique rules?
3. Who maintains eligibility criteria — IT developers, or program officers? (Affects BRE vs Flow decision)
4. Do applicants self-certify eligibility, or does your staff verify?
5. What are the most common reasons applications are rejected at eligibility?
6. Are there applicants who apply for multiple programs simultaneously?
7. Do you have existing eligibility screening tools or does everything go through Salesforce?
8. Are there income, geography, or organization-size thresholds that vary by program?

---

## Review & Scoring Stage
1. How many reviewers does each application go through?
2. Is scoring done by a single reviewer or a panel with averaged scores?
3. Do you have defined scoring rubrics, or is review more qualitative?
4. How long does your review cycle take? (Affects urgency for Summarize / Score actions)
5. Do reviewers need to see related applications from the same organization?
6. Is there a conflict-of-interest check required before assignment?
7. Do reviewers need access to external data (applicant's prior grant history from other agencies)?
8. What's the most time-consuming part of the review process today?

---

## Award & Disbursement Stage
1. How are award decisions communicated to applicants today?
2. Are there legally required timeframes for notifying applicants of approval/denial?
3. Do denied applicants have the right to appeal? If so, what's the process?
4. Is the award disbursed in a single payment or milestones?
5. Are award letters generated automatically or manually?
6. Do you need DocGen for award letter generation?

---

## Compliance & Reporting Stage
1. What compliance reports do grantees need to submit?
2. How do you track whether reports are submitted on time?
3. What happens when a grantee falls out of compliance?
4. Do you need to monitor budget utilization mid-award?
5. How do you handle amendments to award amounts or timelines?
6. Is there a clawback or suspension process for non-compliance?
7. Do you report grant outcomes to a federal agency or oversight body?

---

## Constituent / Applicant Experience
1. Do constituents apply online, by mail, or in person? (Portal vs phone vs office visit)
2. How tech-savvy is your typical applicant population?
3. Do applicants frequently call your office to check application status?
4. What are the top 5 questions your staff fields from applicants?
5. Do applicants need help understanding which program to apply for?
6. Is there a case manager assigned to each applicant, or is it self-service?

---

## Data & Systems
1. Do you have constituent data in other Salesforce orgs or external systems?
2. Is there a housing, case management, or benefits system that shares constituents with grants?
3. Do you want a unified view of a constituent's full benefit history? (D360 signal)
4. Is there a federal grants database (Grants.gov, SAM.gov) you need to check against?
5. Are there document requirements that vary by applicant type or program?

---

## Technical / Gov Cloud
1. Is this org on a FedRAMP-authorized instance?
2. What is your org's instance URL? (Check for gov cloud indicators)
3. Does your agency have a cybersecurity review process for new AI features?
4. Are there data residency requirements for applicant PII?
